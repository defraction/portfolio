About Me

Hwapyeong Yoo

Hello, my name is Hwapyeong Yoo, and I am a freshman at Dublin High currently in 2018. I think that we, as human beings, just want two things in life: Satisfaction and Happiness. We do everything in order to achieve those two things, whether it be being academically successful for a good job that pays well, or finding hobbies and interests that we can work on to improve at. Because of this, I strive to be a person who is satisfied with himself and is able to help others obtain happiness and satisfation for themselves. Whether it be from becoming a programmer to design games that bring enjoyment to people's life, or becoming a humanitarian who can help people in extreme need.

Some traits that I have that will allow me to help people are that I am patient and a team player. From about 9 years ago, my brother was born with a chromosome deletion which put him on the autism spectrum. Living with him for that long of a time led me to develop a extremely long patience which can be utilized to hear people out and give support to them. Also, I always try to work for the good of all instead of one even if it includes self-sacrifice. That will let me work for others and still find my own joy while helpings others find theirs.

In terms of achievements in school, I do not really have any currently, but will continue striving for them. Also, as of now I do not have any work experience built up, but I am planning to possibly start volunteering in a variety of different places to make myself an all-around better and knowledgable person. Overall, I would like to develop my overall general knowledge so I can help people achieve the two fundementals that everyone strives for: Happiness and Satisfaction.
